SynthID Verify explores the intersection of generative AI and digital identity. As synthetic media becomes indistinguishable from authentic content, identity verification systems face fundamental challenges.

Our research focuses on:
- **Multimodal detection** of synthetic faces, voices, and text
- **Provenance frameworks** for tracking AI-generated content
- **Adversarial robustness** of detection systems under adaptive attacks
- **User studies** on human ability to detect synthetic media

We collaborate with identity verification platforms, social media companies, and government agencies working on digital identity standards.
