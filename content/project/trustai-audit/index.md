TrustAI Audit is an open research initiative developing evaluation frameworks for trustworthy AI systems. The project provides:

- **Benchmarks** for measuring LLM robustness, calibration, and fairness across domains
- **Audit checklists** for practitioners deploying AI in high-stakes settings
- **Policy briefs** translating technical findings for regulators and governance bodies

The framework is informed by real-world deployment case studies and collaboration with industry partners and civil society organizations.
